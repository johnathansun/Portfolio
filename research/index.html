Research


Some of the time-lapse imagery that I help to analyze

I’m a volunteer research assistant at the National Institutes of Health.

I analyze intravital microscopy data to help investigate how CCR6/CCL20 chemokine mechanisms regulate immune responses to psoriasis.

Specifically, I try to delineate changed migration patterns of γδ T cells in CCR6+/GFP mice under inflammatory conditions. 

This involves using IMARIS and MATLAB to track 3D displacement and angles, along with quantitative manipulations.
