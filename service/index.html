Paragon Debate

Debate improved my argumentation skills immensely, but the learning curve of formal debate can be steep.

I started Paragon Debate to share lessons from debate in an approachable, fun format. My team and I have taught 150+ kids around the world, from the East Coast to LA to China.

You can find out more at our website: www.paragondebate.org

Tacy Foundation

I gave piano lessons at Gaithersburg Elementary School and played in the region’s hospitals and nursing homes.

I loved working with kids and seeing them get excited about music.

It reminded me of when I was just starting out. The piano can be so tricky and frustrating to learn, but it’s so rewarding to hear harmonious music at the end.

Montgomery County Public Schools
Special Elections Committee

 

I delivered a brief presentation on the impacts of selective voting to the SEC. October 2021

I’m a board member of the Special Elections Committee. We organize county elections for the Student Member of the Board of Education (SMOB). 80,000+ MCPS students vote in these contests.

I am evaluating the viability alternate voting systems for our election (e.g. ranked-choice, multi-round nominations). I also amend protocols for digital voting, nominating conventions, and town hall events.
