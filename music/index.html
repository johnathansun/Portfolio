Piano

I’ve studied the piano for 11 years, and it’s still as fascinating as when I began.

I also love to listen to classical music. Krystian Zimerman’s Schubert Sonata album is on repeat right now. It’s incredible; he constructed his own keyboard to better reproduce the piano sounds of Schubert’s time.
